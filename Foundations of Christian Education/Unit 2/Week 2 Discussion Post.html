3 Things You Learned:

  • Differences between classical conditioning and operant conditioning theories
  • Information Processing Theory and how it pretty much compares a human’s ability to retain information to that of how a computer operates (SR-STM-LTR)
  • System specific teaching principles and when to use them
    • Behavioral when the people you are teaching are unmotivated or have difficulty behaving
    • Cognitive when you are teaching curious minds
    • Affective when you favor personal engagement and the students are self-motivated.
